Our Cremation Services

The Shalom Escort Funerals and Casket Stores (Sefacas) strive to cater to the various needs of all of our clients. We recognize that as a matter of choice, persons are opting to have their deceased loved ones cremated. There are certain prerequisites associated with cremation because it is an irreversible process, and this undertaking can be quite daunting during your time of sorrow.
Our competent and reliable staff, therefore, stands ready to take care of all of the necessary arrangements on your behalf.

Direct Cremation Service

Our Direct Cremation Package includes:

  • Transportation of the deceased from place of death to Cremation facility.
  • Obtaining the True Certified Copies of Death Certificate
  • Filing of Death Certificate and all Necessary Permits
  • Alternative Cremation Container if Required
  • Return of Cremated Remains in our Minimum Urn
  • Crematory fee
  • Transfer of Cremated Remains into a Purchased Urn
  • Social Security Notification

Authorization

Permits and authorizations are prepared according to state law. We also notify Social Security.
