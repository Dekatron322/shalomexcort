The Order Of Funeral Service

Order of Service are processions in which the Funeral Service will run. They are prepared by the Church with input from the deceased family; a copy is then emailed, or brought to the Funeral Home. The Funeral Home uses the Order of Service to prepare the memorial hymnals. The Order of Service includes the hymns/songs, tributes, pallbearers, ushers, photos, and any personal notes from the family printed in hardcopy.
